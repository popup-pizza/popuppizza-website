The Process

From our oven
to yours

We do the hard work, you finish the job. It takes just 10 minutes and the result is a bubbling, golden pizza that tastes like it came straight from the fire.

01

Stone-Baked & Partially Baked

We handcraft each base and partially bake it on a stone deck, locking in a crisp, authentic texture before we even begin topping it.

02

Topped & Packaged Fresh

Your chosen toppings are added generously and the pizza is sealed fresh, refrigerated or frozen until you're ready to eat.

03

Finish in 10 Minutes

Slide it into a 200°C oven for around 10 minutes. When the cheese is melted, bubbling and lightly golden, it's ready. Slice, serve, enjoy.

Ready When You Are

Not ready to eat?
No problem.

Pop your pizza in the freezer and keep it on hand for a quick, delicious meal whenever you need it. Refrigerate for up to 5 days, or freeze for up to 3 months.

Baking Instructions

1

From frozen? Defrost your pizza for approximately 20 minutes before baking.

2

Pre-heat your oven to 200°C.

3

Slide the pizza directly onto an oven tray and bake for 10 to 15 minutes. Ovens vary, watch carefully!

4

Remove from the oven when the cheese is golden and bubbling. Enjoy.

Refrigerate up to 5 days
Freeze up to 3 months
