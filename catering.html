How It Works

Book, confirm, enjoy

Booking Pop-Up Pizza for your event is straightforward, from first enquiry to the last slice.

01

Tell Us About Your Event

Send us your date, guest numbers, and location using the form below. We'll get back to you within 48 hours with availability and a quote.

02

Confirm the Details

Once you're happy with the quote, we'll confirm your booking with a deposit and lock in your date, menu, and any dietary requirements.

03

We Bring the Oven

On the day, we set up on site and cook fresh, stone-baked pizza for your guests, hot, fresh, and made to order.

What's Included

Everything We Bring

Our catering packages are designed to be genuinely all-inclusive, no hidden extras, no surprises on the day.

  • Our own gas-fired stone oven, set up on site
  • Fresh, hand-stretched dough, made from our 72 hr fermented recipe
  • Unlimited pizza for the duration of your event

  • On-site cooking staff for the full event
