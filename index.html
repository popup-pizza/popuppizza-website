Our Story

One Recipe, Two Experiences

Pop-Up Pizza began with a simple belief: great pizza shouldn't be limited to restaurants.

We wanted to create a pizza that delivers the same handcrafted quality whether you enjoy it fresh from our oven or finish it in yours. Every pizza starts the same way: handcrafted dough, slow-fermented for flavour, stone-baked, and topped with premium ingredients in small batches.

Choose it Freshly Cooked, and we'll bake it to perfection, ready to enjoy straight away. Choose Take & Bake, and we'll prepare it so you can finish it in your own oven in around 10 minutes, enjoying that fresh-from-the-oven experience whenever it suits you.

Different experiences. The same commitment to quality.

Because at Pop-Up Pizza, we believe great pizza is about craftsmanship, quality ingredients, and doing things properly, never shortcuts or mass production.
